Delivery Information

At Freshmart, we are committed to delivering your groceries promptly and in perfect condition. Below, you'll find all the details about our delivery process, timelines, and charges.

1. Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to the following regions:

  • Surrounding areas.
  • Urban suburbs within a 20-mile radius of Freshmart.

2. Delivery Charges

Our delivery charges are based on your order value and location:

Order Value Delivery Charge
Orders above $50 Free
Orders between $20 and $50 $5
Orders below $20 $10

3. Delivery Timelines

We aim to deliver your order within the following timeframes:

  • Same-Day Delivery: For orders placed before 12:00 PM.
  • Next-Day Delivery: For orders placed after 12:00 PM.

4. Delivery Policy

To ensure smooth delivery, please:

  • Provide an accurate delivery address and contact information.
  • Be available to receive your order during the selected delivery slot.
  • Inspect your groceries upon delivery and report any issues within 24 hours.

5. Contact Us

Have questions about your delivery? Reach out to our support team:

  • Email: support@foodmart.com
  • Phone: +1-800-555-1234
